I am an avid auto and rickshaw traveller since I am a college student. And I once sat beside the driver , the front seat (I was desperate since I was very late for an exam ) and I swear never to again . Now I leave my house early just to ensure I don’t have to do this again . 

You don’t wanna be cramped up between the driver and some random stranger . Their hands on me unknowingly but mostly knowingly . 

But I often find even men trying to avoid that seat , why , I asked one of my classmates and he said his can’t sit comfortably in the front . And it seriously made me laugh , maybe there is a reason men are told to Man up .

Me and a boy both don’t wanna sit in the front because I am scared of some creepy stranger and he is just uncomfortable . 

The front seat is I think one of the  many  things women don’t have access to . But it changes , it does . When the driver is a female.

Yesterday I came across a female driver , I sat at the back as always . And then when the back seats were all occupied  a man came and now he was flustered as to where to sit , I find it amusing seeing men hesitate . 

But then it occurred to me ,how it was obvious for me to sit at the back just because that is how it has always been , I have been trained in such a way that it doesn’t even matter if the driver is a female, I just sit at the back .

And now I wonder how many back seats women take just because the world is male driven . And how many females still fails to claim the front seat just out of hardcoded behaviour.
